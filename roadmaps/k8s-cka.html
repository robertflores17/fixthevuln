Week-by-Week Study Plan

Week 1

Domain 1: Cluster Architecture — Control plane components (API server, etcd, scheduler, controller manager)

Week 2

Domain 1: Installation — kubeadm cluster setup, RBAC configuration, managing cluster certificates

Week 3

Domain 2: Workloads — Deployments, ReplicaSets, DaemonSets, StatefulSets, Jobs, CronJobs

Week 4

Domain 2: Scheduling — Node selectors, affinity/anti-affinity, taints/tolerations, resource limits

Week 5

Domain 3: Services — ClusterIP, NodePort, LoadBalancer, ExternalName, Ingress controllers

Week 6

Domain 3: Networking — Network policies, DNS (CoreDNS), CNI plugins, pod-to-pod communication

Week 7

Domain 4: Storage — PersistentVolumes, PersistentVolumeClaims, StorageClasses, volume types

Week 8

Domain 5: Troubleshooting — Pod debugging, node issues, networking problems, log analysis

Week 9

Domain 5: Advanced Troubleshooting — Control plane failures, etcd backup/restore, certificate issues

Week 10

Full Review: Practice labs (killer.sh), kubectl drills, Weak areas, Exam logistics
