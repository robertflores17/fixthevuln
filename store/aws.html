Frequently Asked Questions

What do I get after purchase?

You'll receive an instant download link for your PDF study planner. Standard and Dark Mode planners include 55-75 pages depending on the certification, while ADHD-Friendly planners include 100+ pages with additional focus, energy tracking, and reward sections.

What's the difference between Standard and ADHD-Friendly?

The ADHD-Friendly version features shorter study sessions (15-20 min blocks), built-in break reminders, reward tracking, larger checkboxes, calming color palette, and encouraging micro-copy throughout. Same comprehensive content, optimized for focus.

Can I print these planners?

Yes! All planners are designed for US Letter size (8.5" x 11") and look great printed. The Standard and ADHD versions are optimized for printing. Dark Mode is designed for on-screen use but can be printed too.
