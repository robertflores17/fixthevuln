Why I Started FixTheVuln

After remediating hundreds of vulnerabilities across different industries, I noticed a pattern: most companies struggle not with finding vulnerabilities (scanning tools do that), but with fixing them effectively. There's a gap between vulnerability reports and actual remediation that costs organizations time, money, and peace of mind.

This blog exists to bridge that gap. I share practical, actionable remediation strategies—the kind you won't find in generic security documentation. These are real-world solutions that I've tested in production environments, complete with the lessons learned along the way.

My Approach

Security doesn't have to be complicated or overwhelming. My philosophy is simple: understand the vulnerability, assess the risk, implement the fix, and verify it works. No security theater, no checkbox compliance—just practical solutions that actually protect your systems.

I believe in:

Pragmatic Security: Not every vulnerability needs to be fixed immediately. I help prioritize based on actual risk to your business, not just CVSS scores.

Clear Communication: Security jargon helps no one. I translate technical vulnerabilities into business impact and provide remediation guidance that both technical and non-technical stakeholders can understand.

Sustainable Solutions: Quick patches are sometimes necessary, but I focus on long-term fixes that prevent similar vulnerabilities from appearing in the future.
