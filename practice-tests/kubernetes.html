Frequently Asked Questions

Are Kubernetes exams performance-based?

Yes. CKA, CKAD, and CKS are hands-on exams where you solve problems in a live Kubernetes environment. Our practice questions test the conceptual knowledge that underpins practical skills.

Which Kubernetes cert should I get first?

CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator) is the recommended starting point. CKAD focuses on application development, and CKS covers security — both build on CKA knowledge.
