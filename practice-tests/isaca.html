Frequently Asked Questions

CISM vs CISSP — which should I get?

CISM focuses on security management and governance (management-track), while CISSP covers broader technical security domains. Many senior professionals hold both.

Do ISACA exams require work experience?

Yes. CISM requires 5 years of security management experience, CISA requires 5 years of audit experience, and CRISC requires 3 years of risk management experience (waivers available).
